Child Safety Standards

App name: Ganesh Bhise – Social worker

Developer: Rudraksh Jani

Last updated: 9th Decemeber 2025

The safety and dignity of children and young people are extremely important to us. This Child Safety Standards Policy explains how the app “Ganesh Bhise – Social worker” handles child safety, including the prevention of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM), in line with Google Play’s Child Safety Standards and applicable laws.

1. Scope and Purpose

This policy applies to all users of the app and covers all content, messages, feedback, and interactions that may occur through the app’s features. The app is primarily intended for adults to share feedback and views related to the social work and public service activities of Ganesh Bhise.

2. Zero Tolerance for Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

We maintain a strict, zero-tolerance stance toward any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Any attempt to groom, coerce, or exploit a minor.
  • Requests for sexual content involving minors.
  • Sharing, requesting, or promoting explicit or suggestive content involving minors.
  • Threats, blackmail, or extortion involving a child or their images.

Users are strictly prohibited from using this app to harm, exploit, or endanger children in any way. Accounts involved in such activity may be suspended or permanently banned without notice.

3. Prohibited Content: Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

We do not allow any child sexual abuse material (CSAM) on or through our app. CSAM includes any visual, textual, audio, or video content that depicts or attempts to depict a minor in sexually explicit or exploitative situations.

If we become aware of CSAM being shared, uploaded, or reported in connection with our app:

  • We will promptly remove or disable access to the content.
  • We may suspend or terminate the accounts involved.
  • Where required by law, we will report the material and related information to the appropriate authorities or hotlines in the relevant jurisdiction.

4. In-App Feedback and Reporting Mechanism

The app provides an in-app feedback mechanism that allows users to:

  • Submit general feedback about the app and the social work initiatives.
  • Report harmful, abusive, or suspicious behavior.
  • Raise concerns related to child safety or potential CSAE/CSAM.

If you see any content or behavior in the app that may put a child at risk, please use the in-app feedback option to report it as soon as possible. You may also contact us directly using the email listed in the Child Safety Contact section.

5. Handling Reports and Enforcement

We review safety-related reports as a priority. When a report is received, we may:

  • Investigate the content and the accounts involved.
  • Remove or restrict access to content that violates this policy.
  • Warn, temporarily suspend, or permanently ban users responsible for violations.
  • Preserve relevant data for a reasonable time period to support any legal investigation, subject to applicable laws and our privacy commitments.

We reserve the right to take any additional measures that we consider necessary to protect children and comply with legal or platform requirements.

6. Compliance with Child Safety Laws

We are committed to complying with applicable child protection and online safety laws and regulations in the regions where the app is used. This may include:

  • Reporting confirmed instances of CSAM to the appropriate national or regional authority or hotline, where legally required.
  • Cooperating with law enforcement agencies during investigations into child sexual abuse or exploitation.
  • Updating internal processes and this policy in response to legal or regulatory changes.

7. Protection of Minors and Acceptable Use

To further support child safety, the following behaviors are not allowed within the app:

  • Harassment, bullying, or threats directed at children or young people.
  • Encouraging minors to share personal contact details or private information.
  • Any romantic, sexual, or suggestive communication involving or targeting minors.
  • Sharing graphic violence, self-harm encouragement, or other content that may be harmful to minors.

We encourage parents, guardians, and adult users to remain vigilant when allowing minors access to devices and apps and to report any concerns immediately.
